Tybee Jump Rope

A durable, beautiful jump rope. Use it everyday—forever.

The Tybee Jump Rope is assembled by hand out of a combination of turned wooden handles, leather cord, and 550 paracord.

As a last step, a little boiling water is used to tighten the paracord and bind the elements together.

Fabrication

Handle

  • Walnut or maple hardwood
  • 5×1.25″
  • CNC machined in Michigan; laser engraved in Pennsylvania

Leather Cord

  • American leather
  • Variable lengths; 1/4 inch diameter
  • Tanned, heat-treated and curried in New Hampshire

Paracord

  • 550 paracord nylon
  • Variable lengths; 5mm diameter
  • Manufactured in Massachusetts

Final assembly at Dispatches in Brooklyn.
