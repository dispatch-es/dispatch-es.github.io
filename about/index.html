Who/What/Where/When/Why/How

Founded by Dylan Fareed in 2015, Dispatches is a multi-disciplinary product design practice in New York City.

Our mission is is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Short of that, we aim to prototype, manufacture and sell four physical products in a variety of materials each year.

Initially, Dispatches is releasing two products Dylan Fareed designed with Hunter Craighill at GENMFGCO in 2013 and 2014, the Tybee Jump Rope and the Bozeman Coat Rack. We are slowly slouching towards another new product release in January 2016 with additional items every three months thereafter.

We work closely with a handful of American manufacturers to produce objects that uniquely combine utility, handsomeness, and durablity.

We make things that we hope will grow old with you.

Questions/comments/support? Have a read through our shipping policies or email us at support@dispatch.es.
